1. About Compass Place

Compass Place is a mobile application that helps families find senior living facilities for aging parents or loved ones. We provide:

  • Care needs assessments

  • Tour scheduling coordination via SMS

Compass Place is free for families. We earn referral fees from facilities when you move your parent into a facility found through our App. These fees never influence our rankings.

5. Tour Scheduling

When you request a tour through Compass Place:

  • We send an SMS to the facility's designated contact person on your behalf
  • The facility responds with available times
  • We relay their response to you via SMS and/or push notification
  • You confirm your preferred time
  • Both you and the facility receive confirmation

You agree to:

  • Provide accurate contact information
